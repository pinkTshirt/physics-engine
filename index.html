Purpose

Built for correctness before spectacle.

The package favors transparent numerics over black-box behavior. Every example tracks conservation laws, known analytic behavior, or a recognized benchmark, so visual output is backed by measurable checks.

Architecture

Small modules with clear responsibilities

particles.pyarray-backed positions, velocities, and masses
forces.pyNewtonian gravity, uniform fields, and combinations
integrators.pyEuler, semi-implicit Euler, Velocity Verlet, and RK4
diagnostics.pyenergy, momentum, angular momentum, and center of mass
simulation.pythe orchestration layer for stepping and history
